About Us

Rochester City Church is a community of believers serving the Rochester area. We are part of the Buffalo District of the Church of Pentecost, USA Inc. We are committed to helping people grow in faith and be known by our love for one another. Our mission is to provide a welcoming environment where everyone can experience the love of God.
